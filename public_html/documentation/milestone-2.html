Content Strategy

This site will be one layout, with 3 pages/sections:

Welcome (Home)

The index page where visitors will be greeted by a welcome message, a brief overview of my strongest skills, an iconic New-Mexico themed photo (VLA at night).

Portfolio

This page will contain a dynamic carousel that will feature visuals of my design work two items at a time, divided into categories: Identity; Photography; Print; Web Development. It is possible that I may drop or consolidate one or two categories.

Contact

This page offers the user a means to contact me through a form that is sent to my email, and likely some other means of contact via my social media presence.

Wireframes

This pages of this site will feature one layout which will be fully responsive on small and large screens.
